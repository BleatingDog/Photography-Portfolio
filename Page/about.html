Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At Photo Impact, our mission is to preserve life’s most precious moments through the art of photography. We aim to deliver unforgettable memories with professionalism, creativity, and heart.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every special event—no matter how big or small—is beautifully immortalized through images that tell a story, stir emotion, and last forever.

Our Story

Photo Impact was founded over a decade ago with a single goal: to capture timeless, high-quality images that speak louder than words. With over 10 years in the industry and led by our passionate head photographer Mauricio Gonzalez—who brings more than 20 years of experience covering weddings, quinceañeras, private events, and parties—we've built a reputation for excellence, dedication, and personalized service.

What We Do

We offer a wide range of photography services including:

  • Weddings
  • Quinceañeras
  • Birthday parties
  • Corporate events
  • Private sessions
  • Custom event packages tailored to your needs

Whether you’re celebrating a milestone or organizing a formal event, our team ensures every detail is beautifully preserved.
